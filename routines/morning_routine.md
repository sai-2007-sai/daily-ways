# Morning Routine 🌅

## Optimal Morning Sequence (7:00 AM - 8:30 AM)

### 7:00 - 7:10 (Wake Up & Hydration)
- [ ] Wake up naturally or with alarm
- [ ] Drink 500ml of water with lemon
- [ ] Use restroom
- [ ] No phone/social media yet!

### 7:10 - 7:25 (Meditation & Mindfulness)
- [ ] Sit in quiet place
- [ ] Meditation app or guided meditation (15 min)
- [ ] Deep breathing exercises
- [ ] Set intention for the day

### 7:25 - 7:40 (Physical Activity)
- [ ] Light stretching (5 min)
- [ ] 10-minute walk or jog
- [ ] Cold shower (optional but energizing)

### 7:40 - 8:00 (Breakfast)
- [ ] Prepare healthy breakfast
- [ ] Eat slowly and mindfully
- [ ] No distractions (no phone/TV)

### 8:00 - 8:20 (Planning & Goals)
- [ ] Review today's tasks and priorities
- [ ] Set 3 main goals for the day
- [ ] Check calendar
- [ ] Prepare workspace

### 8:20 - 8:30 (Get Ready)
- [ ] Shower and get dressed
- [ ] Grooming and hygiene

## Benefits

✨ **Energy:** Start your day with natural energy and positivity
🧠 **Focus:** Better concentration and mental clarity
📈 **Productivity:** Set the tone for a productive day
😌 **Mood:** Improved mood and reduced anxiety

## Tips for Success

1. **Consistency is key** - Same time every day
2. **Prepare the night before** - Lay out clothes, prepare breakfast items
3. **No screens first** - Avoid phone/email for first hour
4. **Adjust as needed** - Find what works best for you
5. **Track progress** - Note how you feel after each morning

---

**Duration:** 90 minutes | **Best Time:** 6:00-8:00 AM
