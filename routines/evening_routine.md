# Evening Routine 🌙

## Optimal Evening Sequence (9:00 PM - 11:00 PM)

### 9:00 - 9:15 (Wind Down)
- [ ] Stop all work/intense activities
- [ ] Put away work materials
- [ ] Dim the lights
- [ ] Prepare relaxing environment

### 9:15 - 9:30 (Dinner)
- [ ] Eat a light, healthy dinner
- [ ] Eat slowly and mindfully
- [ ] Avoid heavy or spicy foods
- [ ] No caffeine after this time

### 9:30 - 9:45 (Journaling & Reflection)
- [ ] Review today's accomplishments
- [ ] Write down 3 wins from today
- [ ] Reflect on challenges and learnings
- [ ] Express gratitude (5 things)

### 9:45 - 10:15 (Personal Time)
- [ ] Reading (fiction, self-help, or non-fiction)
- [ ] Light hobby or creative activity
- [ ] Stretching or gentle yoga
- [ ] Meditate or breathwork

### 10:15 - 10:45 (Prepare for Tomorrow)
- [ ] Review tomorrow's schedule
- [ ] Plan top 3 priorities
- [ ] Prepare clothes and bag
- [ ] Set alarm clock

### 10:45 - 11:00 (Bedtime Ritual)
- [ ] Skincare/hygiene routine
- [ ] Put phone away (airplane mode)
- [ ] Dim lights further
- [ ] Get into bed

## Important: Screen-Free Time

⚠️ **No screens 1 hour before bed**
- Blue light disrupts sleep
- Stop using phone/laptop by 10:00 PM
- Use an alarm clock instead of phone

## Sleep Optimization

✅ **Do:**
- Maintain consistent sleep schedule
- Keep bedroom cool (65-68°F / 18-20°C)
- Use blackout curtains
- Try white noise if needed

❌ **Avoid:**
- Large meals before bed
- Alcohol and caffeine
- Intense exercise close to bedtime
- Stressful conversations

## Benefits

😴 **Better Sleep:** Fall asleep faster and sleep deeper
🧠 **Mental Health:** Reduced anxiety and stress
⚡ **Recovery:** Allow body to fully recover
📈 **Productivity:** Wake up refreshed and energized

## Tips for Success

1. **Start early** - Begin winding down at 9:00 PM
2. **Be consistent** - Same routine every night
3. **Create ambiance** - Calm, quiet, dark environment
4. **Limit stimulation** - No news, social media, or arguments
5. **Track sleep quality** - Note how you slept each morning
